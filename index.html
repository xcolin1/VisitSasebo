Beautiful Sasebo!

Sasebo City was founded on April 1, 1902.
What is Sasebo Japan known for?
Sasebo City is the central city in the northern part of Nagasaki Prefecture, Japan, and the second largest city in Nagasaki Prefecture. It is second only to Nagasaki City and is well known for its developed shipbuilding and defense industries. Sasebo is surely worth a visit. Head over to the Japanese city of burgers and treat yourself to some juicy meat, stunning sceneries and bustling nightlife!

The Ginza!

What is a Ginza?? The name comes from the words gin meaning “silver” and za meaning “guild”; in 1612 the Japanese government transferred its silver mint to this area. It in turn has become a place of trade and has been transformed into a open air mall, where you can find glamourous and authentic Japanese products


Sasebo, as many Japanese cities and townships hosts one of the more of the most Diverse and Beautiful Ginzas in the whole of Japan. Having a Naval Station located in the Town and a Harbor that see many crusie ships, the towns Ginza has transformed to meet the many different vistors that may find themselves wondering the streets of sasebo. In the Ginza you will find Clothing shops, Resturants, Department stores, Discount shops and many more other shops that can meet your needs and wants. And the best part is that is where most if not all the holiday feastivals will take place!

The Sasebo burger!

Yes! The Sasebo Burger is a thing, and it is DELICOUS! With the prevalance of the Americansliving and visting for work lesiure, burger resturants started to pop up all over the town. The sasebo burger differs slightly depending on the person making it, some use spam and others use bacon of various sizes. But they are all execelent and is a must for any first time visitors!
